The Moomin Museum is open and renewed!
During the spring, we have replaced all paper works with new enchanting works from our collection with Tove Jansson's original illustrations. In Observatorium, a wonderful exhibition has been built with Ilon Wikland's illustration art. Wikland's illustrations are familiar to many through Astrid Lindgren's books and they certainly evoke nostalgia and memories.
You no longer need to book your visit in advance, but we want to limit the number of customers when needed to maintain safety distances. We recommend using a mouth guard in the museum.
So far we have no workshops.
A warm welcome to the renewed Moomin Museum!
